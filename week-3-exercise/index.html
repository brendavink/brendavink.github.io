Who am I? Brenda Vinkemeier


Growing Up

I grew up in Belle Plaine, MN; a small town north of Mankato. I graduated with 70 students.

Living in a small town may have has its advantages for some, but if I could do it all over agian I would have chose a different route. In smaller towns you know everyone and everyne knows you, so privacy and a share amount of rumors and judgemebts tend to be the main topic in the hometown bakery every morning.

After living in Belle PLaine for way too long, getting married and having four children, my husband and I moved to an island in Florida. Moving to a different state showed us all a different perspecitive on life. You can survive in a different state and do not have to commit to living in the place for eternity. This move allowed us to make new friends and gained a son inlaw. Life is too short to stay put, explore the world, there is so much more out there to be seen.


My Family

Family is number one. I no longer live my parents, because I am as old as dirt; well maybe not that old yet. In my fifty years I have managed to be married for 27 years, rasie 4 children and gain bomus kids, which I call my grandsons!
My oldest is a girl, she is married and they blessed me with a grandson named Zachary Apollo (one and half years old). My second daughter is married too and they have blessed me with two grandsons; Brandt Allan (3 years old) and Xander Forest (7 months old). My thrid child, a son is getting married in October and has no children but have two dogs they call their kids, I think it is a gererational thing. Last but not least, my youngest son just bought his first house and is dating a sweet girl, they also have two dogs.
It is strange to think all of my kids have their own homes and our house is empty, but this has its advantages.

  1. Less money for car insurance
  2. No more yearly upgrades on cell phones
  3. Less grocery shopping
  4. Less laundry
  5. Less yelling and arguing
  6. ....and I get to be all of their friends now that I am done rasing them!
